# Ability Examples

### Spell: Trace the Drowned Road
Type: Divination
Cost: 1 Action
Range: Self
Cooldown: 1 Long Rest
Rarity: 2
> The flame stretches into a thread of light and marks the path most recently taken by a soaked creature or wagon.

Properties:
- The path remains visible to the caster for 10 minutes.
- The path ends early if it crosses running water.
- Creatures cannot be tracked this way if they have been dry for more than 1 hour.

Lore:
> Ferrymen once used this magic to guide mourners through flooded grave roads.

### Passive: Grave Road Sense
Type: Exploration
Rarity: 1
> The character feels a cold pressure behind the eyes whenever a road has recently carried the dead.

Properties:
- The character can tell whether a corpse crossed a road within the last 24 hours.
- This sense gives direction, but not distance.

### Effect: Borrowed Last Breath
Type: Curse
Cooldown: 1 Short Rest
Rarity: 3
> A stolen breath fills the target's lungs with words they never meant to say.

Properties:
- The target must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw.
- On a failure, the target speaks one secret sentence chosen by the DM.
- On a success, the target hears the sentence internally but does not speak it.

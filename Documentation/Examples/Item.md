# Item Examples

### Item: Lantern of Still Rain
Type: Wondrous Item
Rarity: 2
Image: angel
> This brass lantern burns with a pale blue flame that does not flicker in wind or rain.

Properties:
- While lit, rain within 10 feet falls straight down and makes no sound.
- Once per long rest, the holder can reveal invisible footprints in wet ground for 10 minutes.
- The lantern goes dark for 1 hour if it is fully submerged.

### Item: Glass Thorn Dagger
Type: Weapon
Rarity: 1
> This narrow dagger looks fragile, but its transparent edge leaves no visible cut until the wound begins to bleed.

Properties:
- Counts as a dagger.
- Once per short rest, the wielder can reroll one damage die after hitting a surprised creature.
- The blade becomes cloudy for 1 minute after this reroll.

### Item: Captain's Weathered Cloak
Type: Cloak
Rarity: 3
Image: p1
> The cloak smells of salt and old smoke. Its brass clasp is shaped like a closed eye.

Properties:
- The wearer gains advantage on saving throws against being frightened while outdoors.
- Once per long rest, the wearer can ignore difficult terrain caused by rain, mud, or shallow water for 10 minutes.
- The cloak cannot dry while its magic is spent.

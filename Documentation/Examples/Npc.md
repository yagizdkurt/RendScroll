# NPC Examples

### NPC: Orren Vale
Personality:
- Polite under pressure.
- Counts every coin twice before accepting it.
Race: Human
Age: 52
Occupation: Gatehouse Clerk
Alignment: Lawful Neutral
HP: 9
AC: 10
Image: p1
First Greeting:
- "Who are you?"
> "If you came for the west road, write your names here and do not linger by the old bridge."
Missing Caravans:
- "What happened to the last caravan?"
> "They paid the toll at dusk. Their horses came back before dawn, still harnessed, but the wagon was gone."
Checks:
- Insight:
> 12: Orren is hiding one detail because he fears being blamed.
> 16: He saw a silver lantern hanging under the bridge the night the caravan vanished.

### NPC: Sister Elian
Personality:
- Gentle, precise, and hard to rush.
- Speaks to frightened people before officials.
Race: Human
Age: 34
Occupation: Field Healer
Alignment: Neutral Good
HP: 18
AC: 11
Triage:
- "Who needs help first?"
> "The quiet ones. The screaming ones still have breath to spend."
The Wound:
- "What caused this injury?"
> "Not a blade. Something pulled heat from the skin until it split."
Checks:
- Medicine:
> 12: Elian has treated this wound before.
> 16: She is worried because the newest wound is colder than the older ones.
- Persuasion:
> 14: Elian shares her notes and asks the party not to name her source.

### NPC: Veyr the Ferryman
Personality:
- Dry humor.
- Refuses to step off his boat after sunset.
Race: Half-Elf
Age: 67
Occupation: Ferryman
HP: 22
AC: 12
Image: Dog
River Crossing:
- "Can you take us across?"
> "Across, yes. Back, maybe. Depends what follows you to the far bank."
Old Debt:
- "Why keep working here?"
> "Because I owe the river one last passenger, and I intend to choose who it is."
Checks:
- Insight:
> 13: Veyr jokes when he is afraid.
- Intimidation:
> 15: He agrees to cross after sunset, but doubles his price.
